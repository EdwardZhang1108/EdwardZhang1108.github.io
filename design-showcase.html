Preface · 2026

A short note on working with systems.

For when you'd like to know what I do, before deciding whether to read further.

I'm an undergraduate at USC, focused on the layer of software where machines stop being a single thing and start being many — operating systems, distributed services, cloud-backed applications, and recent experiments around how models are actually served1. This site is not a CV. The CV is elsewhere. What follows is a slightly longer version, written for people who want to read, not skim.
